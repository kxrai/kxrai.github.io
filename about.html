About Me

Hello! My name is Alicia Loi. I am a web developer with a passion for coding, 3D modeling, and drawing. I enjoy watching anime, especially Naruto, Haikyuu, Your Name, and Miraculous Ladybug. My favorite characters are Naruto, Obito, Korai Hoshiumi, and Luka from Ladybug.

I am also a big sports fan, particularly the NBA and NHL. I admire players like Steph Curry, Jimmy Butler, Kobe Bryant, Anthony Edwards, Auston Matthews, Wayne Gretzky, and Connor McDavid.

Additionally, I love the design of 3D isometric rooms and anime-style art. I have a fondness for Korea and ramen. I am inspired by the design style of Dongkyu Lim.
